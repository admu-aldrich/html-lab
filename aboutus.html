About Digital Espresso

Founded in 2010, Digital Espresso has been at the forefront of combining technology with the art of coffee-making. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where coffee enthusiasts and digital professionals could come together to enjoy premium coffee while staying connected.

Our mission is to provide an exceptional coffee experience while embracing digital innovation in every aspect of our service.

Our Journey

2010

Digital Espresso founded with our first location in downtown Brew City.

2015

Expanded to five locations and launched our mobile ordering app.

2018

Introduced our signature blend and opened our first international branch.

2024

Celebrating our growth to 20+ locations worldwide and launching our sustainability initiative.
